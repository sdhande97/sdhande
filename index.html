ABOUT

My name is Sanket Dhande. I am a Robotics Engineer with a good background in Mechanical Engineering. In 2019, I completed my Bachelors of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from Pune University, India. Soon after with a passion to develop a career in Robotics, I decided to pursue the Masters in Robotics from University at Buffalo, SUNY. During my Bachelors as well as Masters academics I made successive progress and developed expertise in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Robot Operating System (ROS), Manufacturing Processes, CAD and Automobile Systems.

Possessing the above skills I aim to work in the Robotics and A.I field, where I can gain knowledge, and come up with several idea’s to overcome the various challenges while designing and building the Autonomous and Realistic machines and to advance the today’s world one step ahead in this fast growing technology.
