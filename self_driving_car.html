Project Details

  • Category: DEEP LEARNING
  • Libraries: Tensorflow-Keras, OpenCV
  • Simulator: Udacity Car Simulator Enviornment
  • Trained On: NVIDIA RTX 3060
  • Programming Language: PYTHON
Objective: To make a car drive autonomously, by training a DNN model using the images generated by camera located at the center, left and right side of front of the car. Also, to generalize the learned behavior by car on one track to the different tracks in the simulator.

Simulator: Udacity offers a simulator for self driving cars which is a open source platform and works on something close to a real time environment. It is built on Unity, the video game development platform. The simulator consists of a configurable resolution and control settings and is very user friendly. The simulator initially consists of two tracks from which one is quite simple with fewer curvy tracks while other one is complex with sharp turns, shadows and elevations.

Behavioral Cloning technique is used to replicate the human driving behavior which is done in the training mode on the track. Which means the actual data is generated in the training mode in simulator by manually driving the car on the track. Three cameras located at front of the car captures the images of center, left and right side of the track. Simultaneously, the steering angle is also recorded in the csv file at every time step. Using this data the Deep Neural Network models are trained namely 'Pilotnet' and two others 'similar to Alexnet and VGGnet' and their performance are compared with each other regarding how well the model performs on the trained track and how well it generalizes its learning on the other unseen track.

To overcome the challenge of generalizing the behavior of car with other unseen track we do some image preprocessing such as cropping the images and getting the ROI. Also, we implement different Image Augmentation techniques such as Flipping, Panning/Shifting, Brightness and Zoom.

1) Architecture 1 performs well on the trained track as well as it performs quite well on the other unseen track. 2) Architecture 2 performs much better then archit. 1 on trained track but fails to perform well on other track. 3) Architecture 3 performs perfectly on trained track with highest throttle speed and it also performs quite good on other track.
